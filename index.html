The House on the Hill

1084 Road 8 - Powell, Wyoming

Solitude, Views, and Quality

Imagine spending a frosty, Wyoming morning on your deck, sitting next to the double-sided fireplace, sipping your steaming coffee. The majestic Big Horn Mountains are all you see in the distance, illuminated by the winter sunrise. In the fields below, there are a few deer among the trees, and the occasional sparrow breaks the splendid silence with its faint chirps.

Quiet and Comfort

Minutes away from fishing, hunting, or getting lost in the hills, this custom home offers plenty of land (with appx. 4.5 acres of water rights) to bring your horses and all your toys. The upstairs portion has been recently updated, and includes a designer-quality, showcase master bath. The living area is highlighted by a double-sided, brick-enclosed fireplace and plenty of windows for the abundant sunshine. The large chef's kitchen boasts a gas range and steel appliances and ample counter space for all your culinary endeavors.

Custom Touches and Craftsmanship

A one-of-a-kind, steel railing surrounds the stairwell leading to the walk-out basement and second living area. One bedroom has been recently converted into a second kitchen, perfect for guests or mother-in-law quarters. The lower level also offers a high-efficiency pellet stove, perfect for those extra cold Wyoming mornings.
